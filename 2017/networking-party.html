Networking Party

Barcelona Beach - Barceloneta

Location: Passeig Marítim de la Barceloneta, s/n, 08003 Barcelona
Venue: Sal Cafe

We are going to have dinner in Sal Cafe restaurant at the beach of Barcelona. The restaurant is only for us and we will enjoy the buffet food and music.
Dinner will start at 20.00.

We will have 3 buses for about 180 people that will take us from the conference venue to the party place, on Monday at 19.30, based on first-come-first-serve, so please don’t be late.

In the case you prefer to go on your own this is the info that will help you to get there.
